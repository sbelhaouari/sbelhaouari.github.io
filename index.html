Bio

My research interests cover various areas of pure and applied mathematics related to stochastic processes and machine learning. My recent work has focused on data augmentation [KNNOR, KNNOR-Reg, Image Generation], Feature extraction and selection [Radex Transform, Random Feature Explosion, Bird’s Eye View feature selection], Deep learning: Proposing advanced Architecture, optimization techniques, , loss function, and activation function, Outlier detection and clustering algorithms, Probability and Statistics models, Biomedical and Bioinformatics modeling [Visual Signal Analysis, Progressive Fourier Transform], Number Theory and applications in security[Collatz Conjecture to chaos and hash function]

I'm an Assistant Professor in the College Of Science And Engineering at Hamad Bin Khalifa University. Before joining HBKU, I have held several research and teaching positions at Innopolis University, Russia, Alfaisal University, KSA, University of Sharjah, UAE, University Technology PETRONAS, Malaysia, and EPFL Federal Swiss School, Switzerland. I received my Ph.D. degree in Mathematics from the Federal Polytechnic School of Lausanne-Switzerland. I did my M.Sc. in Networks and Telecommunications: Signal/Image Processing from the National Polytechnic Institute (INP/ENSEEIHT), France.
